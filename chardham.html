Char Dham Yatra

Duration: 11 Nights / 12 Days

Destination Covered:Delhi - Haridwar - Barkot - Yamunotri - Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Guptkashi - Kedarnath - Badrinath - Rishikesh - Rudraprayag - Haridwar – Delhi

Trip Highlights

Attend the mesmerizing evening Aarti in Rishikesh Halt at Kempty Falls in Mussoorie Visit to Mana village, the last village before border of Tibet
Camp stays at various destinations Darshan and Poja at Yamunotri temple Darshan and Poja at at Badrinath temple
Darshan and Poja at at Gangotri temple Darshan and Poja at Kedarnath temple Visit to Adi Shankaracharya Samadhi in Kedarnath
Itinerary Details
Day 1 : Delhi – Haridwar
Start your drive to Haridwar form Delhi. On your way to Haridwar, get your Biometric test done for the Yatra.
On reaching Haridwar, check-in to your hotel. In the evening, visit the Har- Ki- Pauri to attend the grand Ganga Aarti on the banks of Ganges. Later, return to the hotel for dinner and have a comfortable stay at night.
Day 2 : Haridwar – Barkot/ Karsali
Day 3 : Barkot/ Kharsali– Yamunotri – Barkot/Kharsali
Day 4 : Barkot/ Kharsali – Uttarkashi
Day 5 : Uttarkashi – Gangotri – Uttarkashi
Day 6 : Uttarkashi – Guptkashi
Day 7 : Guptkashi – Sonprayag – Kedarnath
Day 8 : Kedarnath – Sonprayag – Guptkashi/Rudraprayag
Day 9 : Rudraprayag / Guptkashi – Joshimath – Badrinath
Day 10 : Badrinath – Joshimath – Rudraprayag/Birahi/Srinagar
Day 11 : Birahi/Srinagar/ Rudraprayag – Rishikesh – Haridwar
Day 12 : Haridwar – Delhi
